Chapter 27

Supervised Fine-Tuning

Phase 3: Teaching the model to be a helpful assistant by showing it thousands of ideal conversations.

After base + mid-training, the model is a powerful text predictor but not yet a helpful assistant. It might continue your question with more questions, or write in a random style, or ignore your request entirely. Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) fixes this.

The Chat Format

SFT uses carefully crafted example conversations in a structured format:

<system>You are a helpful, harmless assistant.</system> <user>What is the capital of Tamil Nadu?</user> <assistant>The capital of Tamil Nadu is Chennai (formerly known as Madras). It is the largest city in Tamil Nadu and serves as a major cultural, economic, and educational hub of South India.</assistant>

Human trainers write thousands of these examples covering diverse topics: factual questions, creative writing, code help, math problems, ethical dilemmas, and more. The key constraint: the model only trains on the assistant turns — it doesn't try to learn to generate user messages.

What SFT Teaches

💬

Format

Answer questions directly. Use appropriate structure (lists, paragraphs, code blocks).

🎭

Tone

Be helpful, clear, and friendly. Don't ramble or repeat yourself.

🔒

Safety

Decline harmful requests. Don't generate dangerous content.

🎯

Instruction Following

Do what the user asks. Follow constraints like "in 100 words" or "in Tamil".

SFT is a relatively small amount of training compared to base training — perhaps thousands of conversations vs. billions of tokens. But it has an outsized effect because it teaches the model how to use the knowledge it already has in a way that's useful to humans.

🎓

Analogy: Base training is like reading every book in the library. SFT is like teacher training — learning how to explain things clearly, answer questions helpfully, and be appropriate with students. The knowledge was already there; SFT teaches the model how to deploy it.

Key Insight

SFT transforms a text predictor into a conversational assistant by training on thousands of ideal example conversations. The model learns format, tone, safety, and instruction-following. It's a small amount of training with huge impact — like teaching a knowledgeable person how to be a good teacher.
